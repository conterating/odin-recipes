Chicken Nuggets

Description

Prepare delicious chicken nuggets by cutting boneless, skinless chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces or strips. Coat them first in a seasoned flour mixture, then dip into beaten eggs, and finally coat with breadcrumbs or crushed cornflakes for a crispy exterior. For a healthier option, bake in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through, or fry in oil heated to 350°F (175°C) for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces. These homemade chicken nuggets are perfect for any occasion and sure to please both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut chicken breasts into 1 1/2-inch pieces. Mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan, basil, thyme, and salt together well in a medium bowl. Put melted butter in a bowl or dish for dipping.
  3. Dip chicken pieces into the melted butter first, then coat with the bread crumb mixture. Place the well-coated chicken nuggets on a lightly greased cookie sheet in a single layer.
  4. Bake nuggets in the preheated oven until browned and chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
